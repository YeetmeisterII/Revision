Big O Notation

A method for expressing the complexity of an algorithm.

We can represent this by the notation of \(O(X)\) where \(X\) is some variation of \(N\) such as \(N^2\).

Calculating Big-O Based on Algorithm

  1. To calculate the Big-O for a whole algorithm, break down the time complexity for all the smaller parts of the algorithm
  2. Add all the smaller parts together. If processes occurs within a larger process, add them together and then multiply them by the complexity of the larger process
