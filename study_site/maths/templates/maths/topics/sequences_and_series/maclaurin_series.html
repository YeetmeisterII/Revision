Maclaurin Series

s

Usage

Allows you to approximate a function at a value close to zero.

General Form

For a function \(f(x)\). It's Maclaurin series will be

\( frac{f(0)}{0!} x^0 + frac{f'(0)}{1!} x^1 + frac{f''(0)}{2!} x^2 + \ldots \)
