Planes

Vector Equation of a Plane

If we have three points on the plane \(\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c}\)

We can find the equation of the plane \(\textbf{r}\) = position on plane + \(\lambda\)direction on plane + \(\mu\)direction on plane not parallel to previous direction.

\(\Rightarrow \textbf{r} = \vec{a} + \lambda \vec{ab} + \mu{ac}\)

Equation Using Normal Vector

\(\textbf{r} \cdot \textbf{n} = \textbf{n} \cdot \textbf{a}\)
